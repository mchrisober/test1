Staying awake until you are truly sleepy can help strengthen your sleep drive and encourage sleep to come more easily. Don't know the difference between sleepy and tired? Check out \"Sleep 101\" in the Learn section. Use these activities to help you stay up until your prescribed bedtime. If you are not sleepy at your prescribed bedtime, try using tools to relax your body and quiet your mind. Remember never to stay up when sleepy if it could interfere with activities or responsibilities where sleepiness can be dangerous
